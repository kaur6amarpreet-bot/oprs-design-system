Seat Map

Exact KSRTC 2+1 AC Sleeper layout. Upper berths: 2 columns on the left + 8 columns on the right with an emergency-exit gap before the last column. Lower berths: 10 continuous columns. Each column = one berth position (window side on top, aisle side on bottom). Click a seat to toggle selection.
